I’m a creative designer with a passion for brands and packaging. I’m an inquisitive problem solver, ambitious and love to get hands on with projects, driving them from concept to completion whilst producing creative design solutions along the way.

Over the years I’ve picked up a broad range of design skills along with a knowledge of print processes and an appreciation of the commercial factors that impact design outcomes. I’m experienced in the areas of print, packaging, product and digital design as well as brand and packaging development and project management. I think it’s really important to keep learning - every day’s a school day - so I’m always eager to improve my current skills and add to my skillset too!
